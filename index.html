Environmental Performance Index, 2020 Release (1950 – 2020)

According to SEDAC, the 2020 Environmental Performance Index (2020 EPI) centers on two broad environmental protection objectives:

  1. Reducing environmental stresses on human health, and
  2. Promoting ecosystem vitality and sound natural resource management.

They further report that they are gauged using 32 indicators tracked in 11 issue categories which icludes:

  1. Air Quality
  2. Sanitation and Drinking Water
  3. Heavy Metals
  4. Waste Management
  5. Biodiversity and Habitat
  6. Ecosystem Services
  7. Fisheries
  8. Climate Change
  9. Pollution Emissions
  10. Agriculture
  11. Water Resources
